My Journey

Lessons, moments, and the people who helped me grow.

  1. Finding Strength

    Through the years, I met incredible people. In friendships, and with support from my parents, siblings, teachers—and even myself—I found strength.

  2. Exploring What Matters

    OBMC lets me explore what truly matters to me. From my love of math to my passion for writing, I grew in ways I never imagined.

  3. Speaking Up

    I joined the Poetry Contest three times, despite being shy. Each try made me braver, and I placed twice.

  4. A Home for Expression

    I found a home in the Communication Arts Club—new ways to express myself and share ideas.

  5. A Moment to Remember

    My Grade 6 graduation: receiving my Salutatorian medal and the Poetry Contest medal felt like all the hard work coming together.

  6. What Growth Means

    Being an OBMC student taught me that growth isn’t only about achievements—it’s about what you learn from every experience, every mistake, and every person you meet.

  7. Just Beginning

    This journey is just beginning, and I’m excited for what comes next. …and that is my life as an OBMC student.
